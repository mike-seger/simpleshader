### Audio tracks

The MP3 audio files in `web/media/audio/` are used for shader audio-reactivity demos. All tracks remain the property of their respective artists:

- **Perpetual Overload**, **3 Body Problem**, **Industrial Dreams**, **Between The Worlds**, **Neural Networks**, **Sounds Of Infinity** — from the album *Synthetic Pleasures*
- **80's Synthwave — Synthetic Pleasures** by MOKKA
- **The Gate To The Past (Remix)** by California Sunshine

These tracks are included for personal/demo use only and are not redistributable.

### Tracker modules

The MOD/XM/IT files in `web/media/mod/` are demoscene tracker modules sourced from the [Mod Archive](https://modarchive.org). Each module is the work of its original composer. Tracker music is traditionally shared freely within the demoscene community; please respect individual authors' wishes regarding redistribution.

### Textures

The texture images in `web/media/textures/` are sourced from free PBR/texture libraries and are used under their respective licenses:

- **TextureLabs** — [texturelabs.org](https://texturelabs.org)

### Shadertoy shaders

Shaders in `web/shaders/shadertoy/` are ports or remixes of shaders originally published on [Shadertoy](https://www.shadertoy.com). Each shader file contains its original author's name, Shadertoy URL, and license (typically Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 3.0). Please refer to each file's header for specific terms.

### GPU audio sound shaders

Sound shaders in `web/shaders/shadertoy-gpu-audio/` are ported from Shadertoy. Each file credits its original author and license in the header comment.

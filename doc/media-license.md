- **Perpetual Overload**, **3 Body Problem**, **Industrial Dreams**, **Between The Worlds**, **Neural Networks**, **Sounds Of Infinity** — from the album *Minimal Two* provided by [PandaBeatsMusic](https://pandabeatsmusic.com/)

These tracks are included for personal/demo use only and are not redistributable.

### Tracker modules

The MOD/XM/IT files in `media/mod/` are demoscene tracker modules sourced from the [Mod Archive](https://modarchive.org). Each module is the work of its original composer. Tracker music is traditionally shared freely within the demoscene community; please respect individual authors' wishes regarding redistribution.

### Textures

The texture images in `media/textures/` are sourced from free PBR/texture libraries and are used under their respective licenses:

- **TextureLabs** — [texturelabs.org](https://texturelabs.org)

### Shadertoy shaders

Shaders in `shaders/shadertoy/` are ports or remixes of shaders originally published on [Shadertoy](https://www.shadertoy.com). Each shader file contains its original author's name, Shadertoy URL, and license (typically Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 3.0). Please refer to each file's header for specific terms.

### GPU audio sound shaders

Sound shaders in `shaders/shadertoy-gpu-audio/` are ported from Shadertoy. Each file credits its original author and license in the header comment.
